Use this skill when the task depends on local strategy notes, brand rules, contact tables, calendars, or other workspace files.

Guidelines:

- Prefer local workspace evidence over assumptions.
- Prioritize the newest strategy notes, brand rules, valid contact records, and calendar files.
- Use `find`, `ls`, `grep`, and `read` to locate and confirm relevant evidence.
- When older drafts or signatures conflict with newer planning files, say which source you trusted and why.
- Cite concrete file paths in the deliverable when they are important to the decision.
