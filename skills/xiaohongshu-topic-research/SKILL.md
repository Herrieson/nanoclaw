Use this skill when the task asks for topic selection, audience-fit analysis, or content planning for the creator account.

Guidelines:

- Do not choose a topic from likes alone; consider saves, audience intent, positioning, and brand constraints together.
- Use the local research snapshots under `research/` as your trend reference in this experimental task.
- Prefer topics that match the current account transition toward realistic, budget-friendly lifestyle content.
- When an old high-like topic conflicts with current strategy, explain the tradeoff explicitly.
- Recommend only one primary topic direction unless the prompt asks for alternatives.
